About Us

Our Academy

Located in the heart of Durban at West Street Walk Room 214, Compus Training Academy is dedicated to shaping the future of telecommunications. Our intensive 4-month program combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills, preparing students for successful careers in the telecom industry.

Our Mission

We don't just teach - we build careers. After the 4-month training, we secure 8-month internships for our students, giving them real-world experience that transforms them into industry-ready professionals. We also help qualified individuals with NQF5+ qualifications find meaningful internships.
